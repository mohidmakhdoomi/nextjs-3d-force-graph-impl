## Executive Summary

Implement the specification's selected Stage 1 Group B patch in two phases:

1. reverify the thirteen scoped package actions and their compatibility
   metadata, capture the pre-change audit/tree baseline, update `package.json`
   and `package-lock.json` together under Node `22.23.1` / npm `10.9.8`
   (including the four dev-dependency reclassifications and the `encoding`
   removal), resolve the `@eslint/compat` / Hooks flat-config decision with
   proven coverage equivalence, extend the dependency contract tests, and
   inspect the resulting peer and lock trees;
2. qualify the committed graph through clean installation, static checks,
   production build/start, the existing real-WebGL Chromium smoke, the full
   manual interaction matrix, and final audit-path review with the nested
   PostCSS residual explicitly dispositioned.

The manifest and lockfile remain one rollback unit. No Tailwind 4, ESLint 10,
TypeScript 6, Next 16, 3D-stack, or application-behavior change is planned.
Raw audit JSON remains transient local/CI evidence; durable totals, path-by-path
applicability conclusions, and the nested PostCSS disposition will be written
only to the review document during the Review phase.
